About Us

Farbucks is a coffee shop that was founded on December 02, 2021 in Indonesia. FArbucks was founded by Chikle Jarikle and Roi Dwiwanta. Chikle and Roi are coffee lovers. With the motivation of wanting to convert expenses because of coffee into income because of coffee, they decided to establish FArbucks.

At first, FArbucks only had one shop with five coffee menus, but because it wants to reach a wider market, now FArbucks has opened fifteen new stores with a variety of coffee drinks, decaff coffee, non-coffee.

Our menu is made with secret recipes with extra love from the baristas.
